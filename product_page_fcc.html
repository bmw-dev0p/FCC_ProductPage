Features

  • Apple Intelligence: Powered by the new A19 Bionic chip, the iPhone 16 Pro Max delivers lightning-fast performance and advanced AI capabilities, perfect for gaming, photography, and multitasking.
  • Titanium Body: Crafted from aerospace-grade titanium, this iPhone is not only lightweight but also incredibly durable and stylish.
  • Biggest Display Yet: Enjoy a stunning 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology for smoother scrolling and vivid visuals.
  • Four Stunning Colors: Choose from Midnight Black, Starlight White, Deep Blue, and Sunset Gold.
  • Unmatched Privacy: iOS 18 ensures top-notch security and privacy features, keeping your data safe.

Tech Specs

  • Processor: A19 Bionic with 6-core CPU and 5-core GPU for unparalleled speed.
  • Camera: Triple-lens Pro system (48MP main, 12MP ultra-wide, 12MP telephoto) with 10x optical zoom and 4K ProRes video recording.
  • Display: 6.7-inch OLED display with 3200 x 1440 resolution, 120Hz refresh rate, and HDR10 support.
  • Battery Life: All-day battery with up to 30 hours of video playback and 80W fast charging.
  • Storage Options: 256GB, 512GB, 1TB.
  • Operating System: iOS 18, featuring enhanced widgets and customizable lock screens.
  • Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi 6E, and Bluetooth 5.3.
  • Water Resistance: IP68 rating for up to 6 meters for 30 minutes.

Pricing

The iPhone 16 Pro Max is available in the following configurations:

  • 256GB Storage: $1,199
  • 512GB Storage: $1,399
  • 1TB Storage: $1,599

For more details and to purchase, visit the official Apple Store.
